Data Deletion Instructions

IMM Marketing Hub

Last updated:

Important: You can request deletion of your data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data We Collect

IMM Marketing Hub collects and stores the following data:

  • Social media account credentials (access tokens, account IDs)
  • Content you create and schedule for posting
  • Posting history and analytics
  • App settings and preferences

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Delete Data Through the App

  1. Open the IMM Marketing Hub application
  2. Go to "Settings" or "Account Management"
  3. Click "Delete Account" or "Delete All Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Method 2: Manual Data Deletion

  1. Delete the IMM Marketing Hub application from your device
  2. Remove the app from your social media accounts:
    • Facebook: Go to Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove IMM Marketing Hub
    • Instagram: Go to Settings → Apps and Websites → Remove IMM Marketing Hub
    • LinkedIn: Go to Settings → Privacy → Apps → Remove IMM Marketing Hub

Method 3: Contact Us

If you cannot delete your data through the above methods, contact us directly:

Email: support@immmarketinghub.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request
Include: Your email address and the reason for deletion

What Happens After Deletion

  • All your data will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • Your social media accounts will no longer be connected to our app
  • Any scheduled posts will be cancelled
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account or request data deletion, we will:

  • Delete your data within 30 days
  • Send you a confirmation email
  • Remove all connections to your social media accounts
Note: Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes, but this will be clearly communicated to you.
